Welcome to khanyisa old age home

Our Old Age Home is dedicated to providing a safe, comfortable, and supportive environment for elderly individuals in need of assistance with daily living. With a focus on enhancing the physical, emotional, and social well-being of our residents, we offer personalized care, medical support, and a sense of community. Our mission is to ensure that every resident enjoys a dignified and fulfilling life, fostering independence and well-being through comprehensive services and compassionate staff. We aim to be a place where seniors can find comfort, companionship, and purpose, surrounded by care that respects their individuality.
